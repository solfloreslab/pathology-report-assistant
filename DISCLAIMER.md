# Disclaimer

## Not a Medical Device

This repository contains a **demonstration project** for educational and portfolio purposes only.

It is **NOT**:
- A medical device under EU MDR 2017/745
- A certified AI system under EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
- Validated for clinical use with real patient data
- Intended to replace clinical judgment or professional medical advice
- A diagnostic tool for pathology reporting

## Synthetic Data Only

All pathology reports in `sample-reports/` contain **entirely synthetic, fictitious information**. No real Protected Health Information (PHI) or personal data is included. All patient names, identification numbers, clinical values, and dates are invented.

## Not Clinically Validated

This software has **NOT** undergone:
- Clinical safety assessment (ISO 14971 risk management)
- Conformity assessment under EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
- Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) under GDPR
- Validation against clinical coding standards (ICD-O, SNOMED CT)
- Testing with real clinical workflows or Laboratory Information Systems (LIS)
- Peer review by a panel of pathologists

## Regulatory Position

This project is designed as a **preparatory task** tool (EU AI Act Art. 6(3)(d)) — it structures information for subsequent review by a qualified healthcare professional. It does not autonomously make clinical decisions.

## Usage Warning

**DO NOT** deploy this system in a clinical environment without:
- Institutional review and approval
- Clinical validation with board-certified pathologists
- Regulatory compliance assessment
- Data Protection Impact Assessment
- Integration testing with your LIS/EHR systems
- Human-in-the-loop oversight for all clinical decisions
